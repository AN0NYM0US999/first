Subject= Suggestion regarding Air Pollution Checking

Sir/Madam,
 
Through the column of your reputated newspaper, I would like to draw your attention towards the checking of air quality in big cities like Delhi,Mumbai etc.

 As we know that there is a lot of air pollution Delhi but due to Covid-19, Our air is getting better day by day because everyone is in their home and only a selected 
 vehicles are moving. We should ensure that our air quality should be checked and maintained.
 
 So, I would like to request you to please add a column about this topic.
